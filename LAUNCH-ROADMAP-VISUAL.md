## 🎉 Success Indicators

### Good Signs
- People say "this is cool"
- 5+ installs in first day
- Questions about how it works
- Feature requests coming in
- Someone shares it

### Great Signs
- 50+ stars in 3 days
- Contributors opening PRs
- Featured in newsletter
- Trending on GitHub
- People building on top

### Red Flags
- No comments after 6 hours (expand reach)
- People love demo but won't install (onboarding issue)
- Lots of stars but no engagement (missing community)
