## 🎬 PRODUCTION TIPS

### Recording
1. **Do a dry run** - Record once for practice, delete it
2. **Record in segments** - Easier to edit than one long take
3. **Slow down cursor movements** - Viewers need time to follow
4. **Pause before transitions** - Gives editor breathing room
5. **Over-record B-roll** - You can always cut, can't add later

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Cursor moving too fast
- ❌ Talking too fast (slow down 20%)
- ❌ Not showing UI clearly (zoom in!)
- ❌ Skipping the demo mode section
- ❌ Forgetting call-to-action at end

### If You Mess Up
- Keep recording! Fix it in editing
- Mark the spot verbally ("okay, take 2")
- Don't restart entire video for small mistakes
