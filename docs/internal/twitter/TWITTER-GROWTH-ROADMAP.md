## Course Correction Playbook

### If Growth Stalls (< +5 followers/day)

**Diagnosis:**
- Content not resonating?
- Engagement dropped?
- Community inactive?

**Fix (choose 1-2):**
- Launch new challenge
- Spotlight more users
- Ship requested feature
- Post controversial/interesting opinion
- Engage more actively

**Timeline:** 3-5 days to recover

---

### If Community Goes Quiet (< 5 user posts/week)

**Diagnosis:**
- Not recognizing enough?
- Challenges too hard?
- Lost excitement?

**Fix (choose 1-2):**
- Run easier challenge
- Spotlight 5+ users this week
- Ship fun feature
- AMA / direct engagement
- Reward participation

**Timeline:** 1 week to recover

---

### If Advocates Burn Out (top users stop)

**Diagnosis:**
- Too demanding?
- Not enough recognition?
- Competitors?

**Fix (choose 1-2):**
- Private thank you DMs
- Special perks/access
- Ease their burden
- Recruit new advocates
- Rotate spotlight

**Timeline:** 2 weeks to recover

**If <50 followers on Day 1:**

- Focus on Week 1 activation
- Trading card challenge drives shares
- Consistent engagement > viral moment

**Recovery:** Office tours + spotlights Week 1

### Challenge 2: "Growth plateau at 1200"

**Common at Month 1:**

- Need community activation
- Advocates not recruiting yet

**Recovery:** 
- Launch Discord
- Start newsletter

- Power user interviews

### Challenge 3: "Negative feedback spike"

**When it happens:**

- Promise not kept

**Recovery:**

- Fix fast
