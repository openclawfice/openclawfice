# Twitter Daily Checklist - 30 Minutes

**Goal:** Organic growth through genuine engagement  
**Time:** 30 min/day (10 min morning, 10 min afternoon, 10 min evening)

---

## ☀️ Morning (10 min)

### [ ] Search & Reply (7 min)
1. Search **"openclaw"** → Reply to 2-3 posts
   - Help with setup questions
   - Share your experience
   - Ask about their use case

2. Check **@openclawai mentions** → Reply to 1-2 questions
   - Be helpful, not salesy
   - Share OpenClawfice only if genuinely relevant

3. Browse **#buildinpublic** → Comment on 1-2 projects
   - Give specific feedback
   - Ask thoughtful questions

### [ ] Check Notifications (3 min)
- Reply to anyone who commented on your tweets
- Like/retweet interesting replies

---

## 🌤️ Afternoon (10 min)

### [ ] Post Today's Tweet (3 min)
Pick one from content queue and post:

**Week 1 options:**
- Built a pixel art dashboard for AI agents (intro)
- OpenClaw + OpenClawfice stack explanation
- Gamified interfaces increase engagement 4x (insight)

**Week 2 options:**
- Agent identity files tip (value sharing)
- Water cooler feed vs logs (pro tip)
- Multi-agent setup best practices

**Week 3 options:**
- Trading cards feature launch (building in public)
- Unexpected use cases (user feedback)
- Shareable > useful (viral feature lesson)

### [ ] Engage with Followers (7 min)
- Reply to 5 people in your notifications
- Retweet 1 relevant post with commentary
- Check DMs, respond to questions

---

## 🌙 Evening (10 min)

### [ ] Target Audience Engagement (7 min)
Search one of these terms, engage with 3 posts:
- "ai agents"
- "ai tools"
- "productivity dashboard"
- "dev tools"

Reply with genuine insights, mention OpenClawfice only if helpful.

### [ ] Final Check (3 min)
- Reply to any new mentions
- Like interesting replies to your tweets
- Schedule or queue tomorrow's main tweet

---

## 📊 Weekly Review (Friday, 15 min)

### Metrics to Check:
- [ ] Followers gained this week
- [ ] Reply rate on your tweets (aim >50%)
- [ ] Profile visits (Twitter analytics)
- [ ] Demo link clicks
- [ ] DMs received

### Adjust Strategy:
- Which tweets got best engagement? Do more of that
- Which replies sparked conversations? Refine approach
- Any new use cases discovered? Add to content queue

---

## 🎯 Quick Rules

✅ **Do:**
- Engage 80%, promote 20%
- Use natural lowercase style
- Give genuine feedback
- Reply to everyone who engages with you

❌ **Don't:**
- Spam OpenClawfice links everywhere
- Use the same reply template daily
- Ignore people who reply to you
- Use emdashes or heavy punctuation
