## Tips for Using These

1. **Don't force them** — only use milestone tweets when the milestone is real
2. **Add personality** — these are templates, not scripts. adjust tone to match your voice
3. **Include screenshots/gifs** — visual proof > text claims
4. **Tag relevant people** — if someone helped, tag them (but don't overdo it)
5. **Thread > single tweet** — threads perform better for storytelling
6. **Post at peak times** — 9-10 AM or 1-3 PM EST for best reach
7. **Engage with replies** — milestone tweets get replies. respond to all of them

## Metrics to Track

After each milestone tweet, record:
- Views
- Likes
- Reposts
- Replies
- New followers in next 24h
- GitHub stars gained
- Demo traffic spike

This data tells you which angles work for your audience.
