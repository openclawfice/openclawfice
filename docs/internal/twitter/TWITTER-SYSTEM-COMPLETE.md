## 🎓 Key Learnings

### What We Know Works
1. Multi-message threads > one-off replies
2. High-view placement > random replies
3. Community engagement > broadcasting
4. Trending topics > cold outreach
5. Original content > only replies

### What to Test
1. Different posting times
2. Various content angles
3. Video vs GIF vs screenshot
4. Technical vs casual tone
5. Questions vs statements

### What to Avoid
1. Spamming links
2. Copy-paste without context
3. Arguing with critics
4. Over-promoting
5. Ignoring comments
