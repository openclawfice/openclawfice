## ⚠️ DM Best Practices

1. **Personalize every message** - Reference their actual work
2. **Keep it short** - 3-4 sentences max
3. **Lead with demo link** - Make it easy to try
4. **Don't ask for shares** - Let them share organically if they like it
5. **Respond fast** - Within 1 hour if they reply
6. **Be genuine** - Don't sound like a sales pitch
7. **Space them out** - 5-7 per day max to avoid spam flags

---
