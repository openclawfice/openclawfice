## Tips for Maximum Virality

### Do This:
✅ Lead with the demo link (try-before-install)
✅ Attach a GIF or screenshot (visual > text)
✅ Use emojis strategically (breaks up text)
✅ Thread for depth, single tweet for breadth
✅ Post mid-morning (9-11 AM EST)
✅ Engage with every reply in first hour
✅ Pin the tweet to your profile

### Don't Do This:
❌ Wall of text (break it up with visuals)
❌ Marketing speak ("revolutionary," "game-changer")
❌ No demo link (friction kills virality)
❌ Defensive replies (learn from feedback)
❌ Post-and-ghost (engage with replies!)
