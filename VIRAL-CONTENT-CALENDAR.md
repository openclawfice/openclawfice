## 🎯 Content Principles

### Voice & Tone
- **Lowercase** (casual, approachable)
- **Self-aware** (know it's quirky, embrace it)
- **Relatable** (developer humor, everyday struggles)
- **Enthusiastic** (but not salesy)
- **Authentic** (real founder voice, not corporate)

### Visual Style
- **Pixel art** (consistent with product)
- **GIFs over static** (show motion, status changes)
- **Dark mode** (developer aesthetic)
- **Retro colors** (NES/SNES palette)
- **Text overlays** (context on videos)

### Hashtag Strategy
- **#openclawfice** (brand)
- **#openclaw** (ecosystem)
- **#aiagents** (broader reach)
- **#indiedev** (community)
- **#gamedev** (aesthetic overlap)

Use 2-3 max per post, feels natural not spammy

## 🚀 Viral Amplification

### If a Post Hits 100+ Likes
1. **Reply to top comments** (build thread)
2. **Post follow-up** next day (ride momentum)
3. **Create tutorial** if people ask "how"
4. **Screenshot milestone** and share
5. **Thank community** for support

### If a Post Gets Retweeted by Influencer
1. **Reply with value** (not just "thanks")
2. **Offer demo** or early access
3. **Ask for feedback** (build relationship)
4. **Follow up** in DMs if appropriate

### If a Post Goes Truly Viral (1000+ likes)
1. **Prepare for traffic spike** (server capacity)
2. **Create landing page** specifically for that audience
3. **Write blog post** expanding on the topic
4. **Do AMA** or live demo
5. **Capture emails** for launch announcements
