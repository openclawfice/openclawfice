📸 How to Create Social Card:

  1. Customize the text and gradient above
  2. Press Cmd+Shift+4 on Mac
  3. Click and drag to select the card area
  4. Image saves to Desktop automatically
  5. Upload to Twitter/Discord/Reddit!

💡 Tips:

  • For Twitter: Use 1200×675 (current default)
  • For Discord: Works best with purple/cyberpunk gradient
  • For Reddit: Try square format (1200×1200)
  • Keep text short: 2-3 lines max for readability
  • Test dark mode: All gradients work on dark backgrounds
